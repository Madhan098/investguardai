Indian retail investors face significant challenges including financial fraud (₹5,000+ crores lost annually), complex investment decisions, regulatory compliance confusion, and lack of personalized guidance. 67% of retail investors make uninformed decisions due to information asymmetry and limited access to quality financial advisory services.
Your Solution
SEBI-Saathi - An AI-powered investment assistant that provides:

Real-time fraud detection using pattern analysis
Personalized investment guidance based on risk profiling
Regulatory compliance automation with SEBI guidelines
Market sentiment analysis from multiple data sources
Multilingual support for financial education

The platform integrates live Indian stock market data with OpenAI's advanced language models to deliver contextual, actionable insights to retail investors while ensuring regulatory compliance.
How Will You Use OpenAI APIs?
Primary Integration:

GPT-4o-mini API for conversational investment advisory
Text analysis for fraud detection in investment schemes
Sentiment analysis of market news and social media
Educational content generation tailored to user knowledge levels

Specific Use Cases:

Smart Query Processing: Transform complex financial queries into actionable insights
Risk Assessment: Analyze user portfolios and provide personalized risk warnings
Regulatory Translation: Convert complex SEBI guidelines into simple, understandable language
Fraud Pattern Recognition: Identify red flags in investment opportunities using natural language processing
Market Explanation: Generate easy-to-understand analysis of market movements and trends
